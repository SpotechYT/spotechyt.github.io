Purple F1 | The F1 Rc Car


GitHub: https://github.com/SpotechYT/thepurplef1

For my final project in Circuit Design, my team went above and beyond, fixing and upgrading an old RC our teacher had lying around. We first got it running using the original parts, reparing many broken pices with 3D printed replacements, then upgraded it using a Raspberry Pi to control it using my laptop. After restoring the car, we were able to hit speeds upwards of 40 MPH!

Gaming Wii | A Custom Nintendo Wii


Timelapse: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PCNuCNSvfWs

I found a wii at a garage sale for cheap, and like everything else that enters my hands, I decided to modify it. I painted the case, added acrylic to see the spinning CD inside with some accent lights, and even added a custom firmware with a red launcher theme to match. The wii was my childhood console, and I wanted to give it a new life, in the form of a gaming pc.

Custom Amiibos | Saving money in style


Tutorial: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YOoBAllpzA8

I used to play Breath of The Wild a lot when I was younger, and there were some huge advantages to Amiibos that I wanted without paying the price tag. So instead, I bought cheap NFC stickers online for $1 each and made them into Amiibos using an app on my phone. To top it all off, I 3D printed some custom cases for them to make them look like the real thing, as well as some chip styled Amiibos to save space and keep them with my switch's carying case.
